One of the most common questions we hear at The Lab Barbershop in Manor, TX is: **"How often should I be getting a haircut?"** The answer depends on your style, your hair type, and how sharp you like to look.

## The General Rule

Most men should visit their barber every **2 to 4 weeks**. But that range is wide for a reason — different styles have different maintenance needs.

## Haircut Frequency by Style

### Skin Fades and Buzz Cuts
**Every 1-2 weeks**

Skin fades lose their crispness fastest because the contrast between bald skin and hair growth is immediately visible. If you want that "just left the shop" look, plan on visits every 7-14 days.

### Mid and Low Fades
**Every 2-3 weeks**

These styles have a bit more room for growth before they look untidy. The sweet spot for most of our clients at The Lab is about 2.5 weeks.

### Longer Styles (Textured Crops, Pompadours)
**Every 3-4 weeks**

Longer styles grow out more gracefully, but they still need regular shaping to maintain their structure. Going longer than 4 weeks often means your barber has to re-cut the entire shape rather than maintain it.

### Beards
**Every 2-3 weeks**

Professional beard maintenance keeps your lines clean and your shape proportional. Between visits, maintain with daily brushing and regular oil application.

## Signs It's Time for a Cut

Not sure if you're due? Look for these indicators:

1. **Your fade has a visible line**: The smooth gradient has given way to a distinct step between lengths
2. **Your neckline is fuzzy**: Hair growing below where your haircut ends
3. **Styling takes longer**: You need more product or more time to get your hair to cooperate
4. **Your ears are disappearing**: Hair growing over your ears is a clear sign
5. **People stop complimenting your cut**: Fresh cuts get noticed. If the compliments have stopped, it's time.

## The Cost of Waiting Too Long

Every extra week you wait costs you in two ways:
- **More time in the chair**: Your barber has to re-establish the shape, not just maintain it
- **Less consistent results**: Regular maintenance ensures your barber can fine-tune your cut each time

At The Lab, we recommend setting a recurring appointment. Most of our Manor clients book the same time slot every 2-3 weeks. It becomes part of the routine, and your hair always looks its best.
