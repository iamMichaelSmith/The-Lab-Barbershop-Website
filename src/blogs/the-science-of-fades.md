Grooming at The Lab is an **experiment in structural precision and individual style**. In our Manor shop, we don't just cut hair—we analyze every specimen to ensure the result is mathematically perfect.

### The Manor Standard
Every transformation begins with a detailed profile analysis. We examine your head shape, vertex patterns, and hair density to plan the most efficient transition.

- **Phase I**: Macro-shaping and weight removal.
- **Phase II**: The transition (The Gradient).
- **Phase III**: Micro-alignment and skin-level finishing.

### Professional Instruments
A technician is only as good as their tools. In our laboratory, we use high-torque clippers and sterilized steel razors to maintain the sharpest lines in Texas.

> "True style is found in the intersection of technical skill and visual science."

### Post-Procedure Maintenance
To maintain the integrity of your fade, we recommend a follow-up session every 10–14 days. Our technicians will provide a specific aftercare protocol to keep your scalp healthy and your lines crisp.
