## Why This Matters for Local Clients

In Central Texas, heat, humidity, and fast hair growth can make styles lose shape quickly. The right cut schedule and product routine keep your look cleaner between visits.

## What to Ask Your Barber

Before your next appointment, ask:

- What fade/taper level works best for my head shape?
- How often should I rebook to keep this style sharp?
- Which home products should I use for my hair type?
- What should I avoid doing between appointments?

## When to Book

For most styles, booking every **2-4 weeks** keeps your lineup and shape controlled. If you wear a skin fade or detailed beard work, a tighter schedule can help maintain definition.
